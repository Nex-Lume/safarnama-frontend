Lonavala Adventure: 4-Day Getaway

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Adventure and Relaxation

Start City: Pune

End City: Pune

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Lonavala from Pune. Visit Bhushi Dam to relax by the waters and explore the scenic Lonavala Lake. Overnight stay in Lonavala.

Day 2: Begin your day with a trek to Tiger's Leap and Lion's Point for breathtaking valley views. Spend the afternoon exploring local markets. Overnight stay in Lonavala.

Day 3: Visit the historic Karla Caves in the morning and trek to Lohagad Fort for panoramic views. Relax in the evening. Overnight stay in Lonavala.

Day 4: Spend your final day by the serene Pawna Lake. Enjoy a picnic or water activities before departing for Pune in the evening.

Lonavala 3-Day Escape: Nature and Adventure Package

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Relaxation, Nature, and Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Depart from Mumbai in the morning and arrive in Lonavala (2-hour drive). Check into your hotel. Visit **Bhushi Dam**, where you can enjoy the serene surroundings and cool waters. Explore **Lonavala Lake** in the evening. Overnight stay in Lonavala.

Day 2: Begin the day with a visit to **Tiger’s Leap**, offering stunning panoramic views. Later, trek or drive to **Rajmachi Fort**, a historical fort offering mesmerizing views of the Sahyadri range. End the day at **Lion’s Point** for a breathtaking sunset. Overnight stay in Lonavala.

Day 3: Spend the day at **Della Adventure Park**, enjoying thrilling rides and outdoor activities like zip-lining, ATV rides, and more. After lunch, drive back to Mumbai with wonderful memories of Lonavala.
