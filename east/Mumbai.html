Mumbai Highlights: 3-Day Urban Exploration and Heritage Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Heritage and City Exploration

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Mumbai and check in to your hotel. Begin your day with a visit to the iconic Gateway of India, followed by a ferry ride to the historic Elephanta Caves. In the evening, take a leisurely walk along Marine Drive to enjoy the sunset. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 2: Dive into Mumbai's rich history with a visit to Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) and the nearby BMC building. Explore the Hanging Gardens and Haji Ali Dargah. Spend the evening at Juhu Beach, enjoying street food and local vibes. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 3: Explore Bandra, starting with the Bandra-Worli Sea Link and Bandstand Promenade. Visit Mount Mary Church and the street art-filled bylanes of Bandra. Wrap up your Mumbai trip and proceed for departure with unforgettable memories.

Start City: Delhi

Day 2: Explore Mumbai’s rich heritage by visiting **Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST)**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the **Hanging Gardens**. Mumbai Getaway: 5-Day Urban and Coastal Adventure

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Coastal, Culture, and Relaxation

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Mumbai and check-in to your hotel. Begin your exploration with a visit to the iconic **Gateway of India**. Enjoy a ferry ride to the **Elephanta Caves** and discover ancient rock-cut temples. In the evening, take a stroll along **Marine Drive** for a beautiful sunset. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 2: Spend the day at **Juhu Beach** and relax by the seaside. Visit **ISKCON Temple** nearby, known for its vibrant energy and beautiful architecture. Later, explore **Bandra Bandstand** and enjoy dinner with views of the sea and the **Bandra-Worli Sea Link**. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 3: Visit the **Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Vastu Sangrahalaya**, a historical museum with exhibits on Mumbai’s rich cultural heritage. Explore **Colaba Causeway** for shopping and street food. In the afternoon, visit **Kala Ghoda** for its art galleries, boutiques, and cafes. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 4: Start your day with a visit to the **Haji Ali Dargah**, an iconic mosque located on a small islet in the sea. Head to **Crawford Market** for a vibrant shopping experience and local produce. In the evening, explore the artsy streets of **Colaba** and enjoy a dinner at one of the rooftop restaurants overlooking the city. Overnight stay in Mumbai.

Day 5: Take a scenic drive through **Bandra**, visiting **Mount Mary Church** and **Bandra Fort**. Enjoy a relaxing walk along the **Bandra-Worli Sea Link**. Afterward, visit **Carter Road** and enjoy the local cafés. Depart from Mumbai with unforgettable memories of the city.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
