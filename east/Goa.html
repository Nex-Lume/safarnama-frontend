Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic Coastal Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Goa

Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to Mumbai! Explore iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India and Marine Drive. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 2: Visit Elephanta Caves. Travel to Goa and relax at a beachside resort.

Day 3: Explore North Goa beaches and enjoy thrilling water sports.

Day 4: Discover South Goa's cultural and historical landmarks.

Day 5: Indulge in leisure activities like yoga, spa, and local shopping.

Goa Beach Retreat

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Beach & Leisure

Start City: Goa

End City: Goa

Immerse yourself in the laid-back vibe of Goa’s world-famous beaches. From lounging on sandy shores to indulging in local seafood and exploring vibrant markets, this package offers a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural discovery.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Goa and check-in at your beachside resort. Spend the evening relaxing by the beach.

Day 2: Visit North Goa’s famous beaches like Calangute and Anjuna. Enjoy watersports and beachside dining.

Day 3: Head to South Goa for a peaceful day at quieter beaches like Palolem. Indulge in local seafood and relax.

Day 4: Explore the local markets of Goa, including Mapusa and Panjim. Enjoy the vibrant street markets and shopping.

Day 5: Spend your last morning at the beach before heading to the airport for your return journey.
