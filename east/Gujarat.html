Gujarat Temple Tour: Somnath, Nageshwar, and Dwarka

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Religious, Cultural, Spiritual

Start City: Ahmedabad

End City: Dwarka

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Ahmedabad and drive to **Somnath**. Visit the famous **Somnath Temple**, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Explore the beautiful beaches around Somnath and enjoy the evening light and sound show at the temple. Overnight stay in Somnath.

Day 2: After breakfast, head to **Nageshwar** to visit the **Nageshwar Jyotirlinga Temple**, dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is one of the most significant spiritual destinations in India. Explore nearby attractions and enjoy local cuisine. Return to Somnath for an overnight stay.

Day 3: After breakfast, drive to **Dwarka**, the city of Lord Krishna. Visit the iconic **Dwarkadhish Temple**, a sacred site for Hindus. Take a stroll along the **Dwarka Beach** and enjoy the vibrant local culture. Stay overnight in Dwarka.

Day 4: Visit **Bet Dwarka**, an island temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, located a short boat ride from the main city. Explore the temples and scenic spots. Enjoy shopping at **Dwarka’s local market** for souvenirs like wooden artifacts and local handicrafts. Overnight stay in Dwarka.

Day 5: Visit the **Rukmini Temple**, dedicated to Lord Krishna’s wife, Rukmini, located near Dwarka. Later, head back to Ahmedabad for a brief city tour or shopping before your departure. Enjoy a final evening in Gujarat with traditional cuisine. Overnight stay in Ahmedabad.

Day 6: Departure from Ahmedabad with unforgettable memories of your Gujarat temple tour.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Gujarat Wildlife Safari Tour

Duration: 6 Nights / 7 Days

Type of Trip: Wildlife Safari, Nature Exploration

Start City: Ahmedabad

End City: Ahmedabad

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Ahmedabad and drive to **Gir National Park**. Upon arrival, check into your wildlife resort and enjoy an evening safari in the park. This park is famous for its Asiatic Lions, so keep an eye out for these majestic animals. Overnight stay in Gir.

Day 2: Early morning safari in **Gir National Park** for a closer view of Asiatic Lions and other wildlife like leopards, spotted deer, and sambar. Return for breakfast, followed by an afternoon safari in the park to explore more of the diverse ecosystem. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Gir.

Day 3: After breakfast, drive to **Velavadar Blackbuck National Park**, known for its grasslands and rich birdlife, including large flocks of cranes and storks. The park is also home to the blackbuck and Indian wolves. Overnight stay near Velavadar.

Day 4: Morning safari in **Velavadar National Park**. Explore the vast grasslands and witness herds of blackbucks and the graceful Indian wolves. Afternoon at leisure, with an optional birdwatching session. Overnight stay near the park.

Day 5: After breakfast, drive to the **Rann of Kutch**, famous for its white salt desert landscape. Visit the **Kutch Wildlife Sanctuary** and explore the desert ecosystem, home to migratory birds, wild asses, and more. Enjoy a cultural evening in the desert with traditional music and dance. Overnight stay in Rann of Kutch.

Day 6: Early morning safari in **Kutch Wildlife Sanctuary**, known for its rich biodiversity, including the endangered **Wild Ass**. After lunch, visit **Banni Grasslands** for birdwatching, including flamingos and other migratory species. In the evening, return to the resort and enjoy a local dinner. Overnight stay in Rann of Kutch.

  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.

Romantic Escape to Gujarat

Duration: 6 Nights / 7 Days

Type of Trip: Romantic, Cultural, and Beachside

Start City: Ahmedabad

End City: Ahmedabad

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Ahmedabad. Transfer to **Dwarka**. Relax and enjoy a romantic dinner by the serene coastal atmosphere. Overnight stay in Dwarka.

Day 2: Visit the ancient **Dwarkadhish Temple**. Explore **Bet Dwarka**, known for its peaceful beaches and beautiful sunset views. Enjoy a peaceful boat ride. Overnight stay in Dwarka.

Day 3: Proceed to **Somnath**. Visit the **Somnath Temple**, a revered pilgrimage site. Take a walk along the tranquil **Somnath Beach** and enjoy a peaceful evening. Overnight stay in Somnath.

Day 4: Drive to **Rann of Kutch**. Visit the white desert during sunset and enjoy the unique landscape. If visiting in winter, experience the **Rann Utsav** festival, where you can enjoy cultural performances and craft shopping. Overnight stay in Kutch.

Day 5: Explore **Kutch's handicrafts** and attend a traditional dance performance. Visit **Mandvi Beach** for a romantic stroll. Spend the evening under the stars in the desert camp. Overnight stay in Kutch.

Day 6: Drive back to Ahmedabad. Explore the **Sabarmati Riverfront** for a romantic evening. Visit **Adalaj Stepwell** and enjoy a peaceful boat ride at the **Kankaria Lake**. Overnight stay in Ahmedabad.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
