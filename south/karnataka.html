Karnataka Highlights: A 5-Day Adventure

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Historical, and Scenic Exploration

Start City: Bangalore

End City: Bangalore

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Bangalore. Explore landmarks such as Bangalore Palace, Lalbagh Botanical Garden, and Cubbon Park. Overnight stay in Bangalore.

Day 2: Drive to Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Explore the Vijayanagara ruins, Virupaksha Temple, and Hampi Bazaar. Overnight stay in Hampi.

Day 3: Travel to Coorg, also known as the "Scotland of India." Visit coffee plantations, Abbey Falls, and Raja’s Seat. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Day 4: Head to Mysore. Visit the Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill Temple, and St. Philomena’s Church. Enjoy the Brindavan Gardens light show. Overnight stay in Mysore.

Day 5: Drive to Shivanasamudra Falls. Enjoy the stunning waterfalls and serene surroundings. Return to Bangalore for departure.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Mumbai to Karnataka: A 3-Day Cultural and Scenic Escape

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Heritage, and Nature

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly or take an overnight train from Mumbai to Mysore. Visit the iconic Mysore Palace, St. Philomena’s Church, and enjoy the evening light and sound show at Brindavan Gardens. Overnight stay in Mysore.

Day 2: Start the day with a visit to Chamundi Hill Temple. Later, explore the historical town of Srirangapatna, including the Dariya Daulat Bagh and Tipu Sultan’s Summer Palace. Spend the evening shopping for silk and handicrafts. Overnight stay in Mysore.

Day 3: Visit Shivanasamudra Falls, a stunning waterfall near Mysore. Enjoy the serene surroundings before returning to Mysore to catch your evening flight or train back to Mumbai.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.

Karnataka Highlights: Nature, Heritage & Adventure

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Nature, and Adventure

Start City: Bengaluru

End City: Bengaluru

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Bengaluru. Explore landmarks such as Bengaluru Palace, Cubbon Park, and Lalbagh Botanical Garden. Overnight stay in Bengaluru.

Day 2: Drive to Mysuru (3 hours). Visit the iconic Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill Temple, and St. Philomena’s Church. Enjoy an evening at Brindavan Gardens. Overnight stay in Mysuru.

Day 3: Travel to Coorg (4 hours), known as the Scotland of India. Explore coffee plantations, Abbey Falls, and Raja’s Seat. Overnight stay in Coorg.

Day 4: Visit Dubare Elephant Camp to interact with and bathe elephants. Later, enjoy a scenic river rafting experience on the Kaveri River. Return to Coorg for an overnight stay.

Day 5: En route to Bengaluru, stop at Srirangapatna to explore Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace and Ranganathaswamy Temple. Depart from Bengaluru with cherished memories of Karnataka.

Additional Highlights
