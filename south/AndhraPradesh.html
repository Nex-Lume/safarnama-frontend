Enchanting Andhra Pradesh Getaway

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural and Scenic Exploration

Start City: Visakhapatnam

End City: Visakhapatnam

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Visakhapatnam. Spend the day at Rushikonda Beach, followed by a visit to the Kailasagiri Hilltop Park for panoramic views of the coastline. Overnight stay in Visakhapatnam.

Day 2: Take a scenic train or road trip to Araku Valley. Visit Borra Caves, known for stunning limestone formations. Explore coffee plantations and tribal museums. Return to Visakhapatnam for the night.

Day 3: Visit the historic Simhachalam Temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha. Spend some time exploring the local markets for souvenirs before departing from Visakhapatnam.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Divine Pilgrimage: Mallikarjun & Tirupati

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Pilgrimage

Start City: Hyderabad

End City: Tirupati

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Hyderabad. Drive to Srisailam and visit the Mallikarjun Jyotirling Temple. Overnight stay in Srisailam.

Day 2: Explore nearby attractions such as the Srisailam Dam and Patala Ganga. Evening temple visit for aarti. Overnight in Srisailam.

Day 3: Drive to Tirupati. Relax and prepare for the next day's visit to Tirumala.

Day 4: Visit the iconic Tirupati Balaji Temple at Tirumala. Participate in the darshan and rituals. Overnight in Tirupati.

Day 5: Explore local attractions like the Padmavathi Temple and Tirupati markets. Spend time in serene surroundings.

Day 6: Departure from Tirupati with divine blessings and cherished memories.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
