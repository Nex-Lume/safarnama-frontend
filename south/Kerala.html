Family Fun Kerala Backwater Retreat

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Family Adventure and Cultural Experience

Start City: Kochi

End City: Alleppey

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kochi and check-in to a family-friendly resort. Explore Fort Kochi’s heritage sites and enjoy Kathakali performances.

Day 2: Experience a houseboat cruise through the serene backwaters of Alleppey. Enjoy activities like fishing and bird watching.

Day 3: Drive to Munnar and visit lush tea gardens. Enjoy family-friendly hikes and visit a tea museum.

Day 4: Explore the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary. Go on a family safari and enjoy a boat ride on Periyar Lake.

Day 5: Relax at Varkala Beach with family-friendly activities like beach games and swimming.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.

  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
