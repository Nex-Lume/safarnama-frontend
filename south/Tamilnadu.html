Tamil Nadu: 3-Day Meenakshi, Rameshwaram, and Chennai Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual, Cultural, and Heritage Exploration

Start City: Chennai

End City: Chennai

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Chennai and take a domestic flight or train to Madurai. Visit the iconic **Meenakshi Amman Temple**, famous for its stunning Dravidian architecture and intricate sculptures. Explore the vibrant local markets and enjoy authentic South Indian cuisine. Overnight stay in Madurai.

Day 2: Early morning drive to Rameshwaram. Visit the sacred **Ramanathaswamy Temple**, known for its long corridors and holy wells. Proceed to **Dhanushkodi**, the ghost town at the southern tip of India, and enjoy the serene beaches. Return to Madurai for an overnight stay.

Day 3: Travel back to Chennai. Visit **Kapaleeshwarar Temple**, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and explore **Santhome Basilica** and Marina Beach. Enjoy some last-minute shopping in T. Nagar before your departure from Chennai.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Bengaluru to Rameshwaram: 3-Day Spiritual Temple Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual and Temple Exploration

Start City: Bengaluru

End City: Bengaluru

Itinerary

Day 1: Board an overnight train or early morning flight from Bengaluru to Rameshwaram. Upon arrival, check in to your hotel. Begin your spiritual journey by visiting the **Ramanathaswamy Temple**, renowned for its stunning corridors and 22 sacred wells for purification rituals. Spend the evening at leisure exploring the local markets. Overnight stay in Rameshwaram.

Day 2: Visit **Dhanushkodi**, the ghost town at the tip of India, known for its breathtaking views and spiritual significance. Proceed to **Agni Theertham**, a sacred beach for holy dips. Explore the **Panchmukhi Hanuman Temple** and **Adam’s Bridge** (Ram Setu viewpoint). In the evening, enjoy a tranquil sunset at Pamban Bridge. Overnight stay in Rameshwaram.

Day 3: Begin your day with a temple visit for early morning prayers. After breakfast, check out from the hotel and proceed to the railway station or airport for your return journey to Bengaluru, carrying blessings and cherished memories of a spiritual journey.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.

Tamil Nadu: 3-Day Ooty and Kodaikanal Hill Stations Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Nature Exploration, and Relaxation

Start City: Coimbatore

End City: Coimbatore

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive at Coimbatore and drive to Ooty, the "Queen of Hill Stations." Visit the **Botanical Gardens**, **Ooty Lake**, and **Doddabetta Peak** for panoramic views. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring local markets. Overnight stay in Ooty.

Day 2: After breakfast, drive to Kodaikanal, the "Princess of Hill Stations." En route, enjoy scenic views of the Nilgiri hills. In Kodaikanal, visit **Coaker’s Walk**, **Bryant Park**, and **Silver Cascade Falls**. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.

Day 3: Start the day with a serene walk at **Kodai Lake**. Visit **Pillar Rocks**, **Green Valley View**, and **Pine Forests**. Later, drive back to Coimbatore for your departure with memories of the charming hill stations.
