Heritage and Culture Kolkata Tour

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural and Heritage Tour

Start City: Kolkata

End City: Kolkata

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kolkata and check-in to your hotel. Visit iconic landmarks like Howrah Bridge and the Victoria Memorial.

Day 2: Visit the famous New Market, Kalighat Temple, and explore the vibrant street life of Kolkata.

Day 3: Spend the day at the Indian Museum and explore cultural experiences such as Bengali theater and local art galleries.

Day 4: Take a day trip to Dakshineswar Kali Temple and explore the surrounding peaceful areas along the Hooghly River.

Day 5: Relax at Eden Gardens or take a sunset boat ride on the Hooghly River before your departure.

Romantic Kolkata Escape

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Romantic Getaway and Cultural Exploration

Start City: Kolkata

End City: Kolkata

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kolkata and check-in to your romantic hotel. Start your adventure by visiting the iconic Victoria Memorial and explore its beautiful gardens. Enjoy a romantic sunset boat ride on the Hooghly River, gazing at Howrah Bridge.

Day 2: Explore the hidden gems of Kolkata with a private tour of Marble Palace, an architectural beauty. Later, enjoy a romantic walk through College Street and have lunch at a cozy Bengali café. In the evening, enjoy a candlelight dinner at one of Kolkata's renowned rooftop restaurants, offering stunning views of the city.

Day 3: Spend the day relaxing in the lush greenery of Eden Gardens, with a peaceful picnic in the park. Afterward, visit the charming South Park Street Cemetery for a quiet and intimate experience. Conclude your romantic Kolkata journey with a farewell dinner at an elegant riverside restaurant, watching the lights shimmer on the Hooghly.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
