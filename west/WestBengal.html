West Bengal: 3-Day Kolkata, Mayapur, and Bishnupur Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Spiritual, and Heritage Exploration

Start City: Kolkata

End City: Kolkata

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kolkata and check in to your hotel. Start the day by visiting **Belur Math**, the headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission. Continue to **Dakshineswar Kali Temple**, a significant spiritual landmark. Spend the evening strolling along the Hooghly River at Princep Ghat and enjoy a serene boat ride. Overnight stay in Kolkata.

Day 2: After breakfast, drive to **Mayapur** (approx. 3-4 hours), the headquarters of ISKCON and a major Vaishnavite pilgrimage site. Visit the magnificent **Chandrodaya Mandir** and participate in spiritual activities. Return to Kolkata in the evening and enjoy local Bengali delicacies. Overnight stay in Kolkata.

Day 3: Take an early morning drive to **Bishnupur** (approx. 4 hours), known for its terracotta temples and Baluchari sarees. Explore the **Rasmancha**, **Jor Bangla Temple**, and **Madan Mohan Temple**, showcasing exquisite terracotta art. Afterward, return to Kolkata for your departure, carrying memories of West Bengal’s heritage and spirituality.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Kolkata and Shantiniketan: 3-Day Cultural and Spiritual Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Spiritual, and Heritage Exploration

Start City: Kolkata

End City: Kolkata

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kolkata and check in to your hotel. Begin your journey with a visit to the **Victoria Memorial**, an iconic structure of British-era architecture. Explore **Dakshineswar Kali Temple** and take a boat ride on the Hooghly River to **Belur Math**, the headquarters of the Ramakrishna Mission. In the evening, enjoy local street food and a tram ride. Overnight stay in Kolkata.

Day 2: After breakfast, drive to Shantiniketan (approx. 3-4 hours), the cultural hub founded by Rabindranath Tagore. Visit **Visva-Bharati University**, **Tagore's Ashram**, and the **Rabindra Bhavan Museum**. Stroll through the serene surroundings and witness local crafts at the handicraft markets. Return to Kolkata in the evening. Overnight stay in Kolkata.

Day 3: Start your day with a visit to **Kumartuli**, the artisan hub famous for clay idols. Explore the historical **Marble Palace** and the spiritual **St. Paul’s Cathedral**. Later, shop for souvenirs at **New Market**. After lunch, check out and proceed to the railway station or airport for your onward journey, taking home memories of West Bengal’s rich culture and heritage.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

West Bengal: 3-Day Darjeeling and Kalimpong Hill Stations Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Nature Exploration, and Relaxation

Start City: Siliguri

End City: Siliguri

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive at Siliguri and drive to Darjeeling, the "Queen of Hills." Visit the **Tiger Hill** for a mesmerizing sunrise view over Mt. Kanchenjunga. Explore the **Batasia Loop** and **Ghoom Monastery**. Spend the evening strolling through the famous Mall Road. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 2: After breakfast, drive to Kalimpong, known for its serene ambiance. Visit **Deolo Hill**, **Dr. Graham's Homes**, and **Pine View Nursery** for its exotic cacti collection. Enjoy panoramic views of the Teesta River. Overnight stay in Kalimpong.

Day 3: Start the day with a visit to the **Durpin Monastery** and **Kalimpong Science City**. Enjoy some local shopping for handicrafts and tea. Later, drive back to Siliguri for your departure with unforgettable memories of the charming hill stations.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
