Enchanting Arunachal Pradesh Journey

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic Adventure and Cultural Experience

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Guwahati. Begin your journey with a drive to Bhalukpong, the gateway to Arunachal Pradesh. Overnight stay in Bhalukpong.

Day 2: Drive to Dirang, known for its lush landscapes. Visit Dirang Dzong and Hot Springs. Overnight stay in Dirang.

Day 3: Travel to Tawang, the land of monasteries. Visit the Sela Pass, Jaswant Garh War Memorial, and enjoy the breathtaking views. Overnight stay in Tawang.

Day 4: Visit the famous Tawang Monastery and the serene Madhuri Lake. Explore Bumla Pass (near the India-China border). Overnight stay in Tawang.

Day 5: Drive to Bomdila, known for its apple orchards and monasteries. Visit the Bomdila Monastery and the local craft centers. Overnight stay in Bomdila.

Day 6: Drive back to Guwahati. Visit the Kamakhya Temple if time permits. Fly back to Mumbai with unforgettable memories of Arunachal Pradesh.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Serene Arunachal Adventure

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic and Cultural Exploration

Start City: Guwahati

End City: Guwahati

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Guwahati. Drive to Bhalukpong, the gateway to Arunachal Pradesh. Explore the lush surroundings and enjoy the Kameng River. Overnight stay in Bhalukpong.

Day 2: Drive to Dirang, a picturesque valley surrounded by apple orchards and hot springs. Visit Dirang Monastery and enjoy the scenic views. Overnight stay in Dirang.

Day 3: Drive to Tawang via the spectacular Sela Pass. Visit the Sela Lake, Jaswant Garh War Memorial, and the Nuranang Waterfalls on the way. Overnight stay in Tawang.

Day 4: Visit Tawang Monastery, the largest in India. Explore Bumla Pass and Madhuri Lake for mesmerizing landscapes. Enjoy local culture and food. Overnight stay in Tawang.

Day 5: Drive back to Guwahati with scenic stops. Optionally visit the Kamakhya Temple before departure with cherished memories of Arunachal Pradesh.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Quick Arunachal Getaway

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic and Cultural Highlights

Start City: Guwahati

End City: Guwahati

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Delhi to Guwahati in the morning. Drive to Bhalukpong (approx. 5 hours). Visit Nameri National Park for a nature walk or birdwatching. Overnight stay in Bhalukpong.

Day 2: Drive to Dirang (approx. 4 hours). Explore Dirang Valley, visit Dirang Dzong (fort), and take a dip in the hot springs. Optionally, visit Sangti Valley for its serene beauty. Overnight stay in Dirang.

Day 3: Drive back to Guwahati. Visit Kamakhya Temple and enjoy some local shopping. Take an evening flight back to Delhi.
