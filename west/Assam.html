Assam Heritage and Wilderness Tour

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Wildlife, and Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Guwahati. Upon arrival, enjoy a city tour, visiting attractions like Umananda Temple and the Brahmaputra Riverfront. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 2: Drive to Kaziranga National Park (approx. 4 hours). Enjoy a jeep safari in the park to spot the famous one-horned rhinoceros. Overnight stay in Kaziranga.

Day 3: After breakfast, explore the rich biodiversity of Kaziranga. Later, drive to Guwahati and visit the Kamakhya Temple, an ancient shrine on Nilachal Hill. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 4: Drive to Majuli Island (approx. 5 hours). Explore this unique river island known for its Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries) and vibrant culture. Overnight stay on Majuli Island.

Day 5: Visit Sivasagar to explore its historic monuments, including Rang Ghar and Talatal Ghar. Continue to Jorhat for an evening tea garden visit. Overnight stay in Jorhat.

Day 6: Enjoy a final Brahmaputra River cruise. Visit local markets for souvenirs. Fly back to Mumbai with unforgettable memories of Assam.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Exploring the Heart of Assam

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Wildlife, Culture, and Scenic Exploration

Start City: Guwahati

End City: Guwahati

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Guwahati. Enjoy a Brahmaputra River cruise and visit Umananda Temple. Explore the local markets and enjoy Assamese cuisine. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 2: Travel to Kaziranga National Park (approx. 4 hours). Embark on a jeep safari to spot one-horned rhinos, wild buffaloes, and other wildlife. Overnight stay in Kaziranga.

Day 3: Drive back to Guwahati and visit the historic **Kamakhya Temple** on Nilachal Hill. Spend the evening at **Brahmaputra Riverfront**. Overnight stay in Guwahati.

Day 4: Drive to Majuli Island (approx. 5 hours). Visit the ancient Satras and learn about Assamese culture, art, and history. Overnight stay in Majuli.

Day 5: Head to **Tezpur** to visit the historical sites, including **Agni Mandir** and **Mahabhairab Temple**. Enjoy the scenic beauty around **Sonal Sagar** and **Nameri National Park**. Overnight stay in Tezpur.

Day 6: Take a last river cruise on the Brahmaputra. Explore **local markets** for handicrafts and souvenirs before departing for Guwahati airport with cherished memories.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
