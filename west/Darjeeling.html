Darjeeling Heritage and Adventure Tour

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Cultural, and Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Bagdogra. Drive to Darjeeling (approx. 3 hours). Evening visit to **Peace Pagoda** and **Japanese Temple**. Explore **Mall Road** and enjoy local cuisine. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 2: Early morning visit to **Tiger Hill** for a breathtaking sunrise view of **Kangchenjunga**. Visit **Ghoom Monastery** and the **Batasia Loop**. Enjoy a scenic **Toy Train Ride**. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3: Visit the **Himalayan Mountaineering Institute**, **Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park**, and **Lloyd Botanical Garden**. Spend the evening exploring **Darjeeling’s local markets**. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Visit the **Darjeeling Tea Gardens** and learn about the tea cultivation process. Explore the **Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre** for a cultural experience. Free time for shopping or enjoying scenic views. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 5: Explore **Mahakal Temple** and **Rock Garden**. In the afternoon, drive back to Bagdogra. Fly from Bagdogra to Mumbai, carrying home unforgettable memories of Darjeeling.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Darjeeling from Gujarat

Start City: Ahmedabad (Gujarat)

Day 2: Early morning visit to **Tiger Hill** for a stunning sunrise view of **Kangchenjunga**. Visit **Ghoom Monastery**, **Batasia Loop**, and enjoy a **Toy Train ride** on the **Darjeeling Himalayan Railway**. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 3: Visit the **Himalayan Mountaineering Institute**, explore the **Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park**, and enjoy the serenity of **Lloyd Botanical Garden**. Free time for shopping and exploring the local markets. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 4: Visit the **Darjeeling Tea Gardens** and learn about tea cultivation and processing. Explore **Tibetan Refugee Self-Help Centre** to experience Tibetan culture. Enjoy a relaxing evening at **Neora Valley National Park**. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.

Day 5: Enjoy the day visiting **Mahakal Temple**, **Rock Garden**, and other local landmarks. In the evening, drive back to Bagdogra for your return flight to Ahmedabad with unforgettable memories.
