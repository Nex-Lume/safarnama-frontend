Jagannath Festival Experience: 3-Day Odisha Cultural Journey

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural & Religious Exploration

Start City: Bhubaneswar

End City: Bhubaneswar

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Bhubaneswar. Visit the Lingaraj Temple and explore Udayagiri and Khandagiri Caves. Overnight stay in Bhubaneswar.

Day 2: Early morning visit to Jagannath Temple in Puri. Experience the festival preparations, and spend the evening enjoying cultural shows. Overnight stay in Puri.

Day 3: Witness the grand Jagannath Festival procession. Enjoy traditional Odia cuisine and cultural festivities. Return to Bhubaneswar in the afternoon and depart with memorable experiences.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Odisha Cultural & Heritage Tour: 5-Day Exploration

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Heritage, and Nature Exploration

Start City: Bhubaneswar

End City: Bhubaneswar

Itinerary

Day 2: Travel to Puri. Visit Jagannath Temple, followed by relaxation at Puri Beach. Overnight stay in Puri.

Day 3: Explore Konark Sun Temple, followed by a boat ride at Chilika Lake. Overnight stay in Puri.

Day 4: Travel to Sambalpur. Visit Hirakud Dam and explore local markets and handlooms. Overnight stay in Sambalpur.

Day 5: Visit Dhauli Hill, a peaceful spot with Buddhist stupas. Return to Bhubaneswar and depart for your onward journey.

Mumbai to Odisha: 3-Day Cultural and Heritage Exploration

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural and Heritage Exploration

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Bhubaneswar from Mumbai. Visit the Lingaraj Temple, one of the oldest and largest temples in Odisha. Explore other nearby temples like Mukteswara Temple. Overnight stay in Bhubaneswar.

Day 2: Travel to Puri, visit the famous Jagannath Temple and explore the religious and cultural heritage of the town. Spend the evening relaxing at Puri Beach. Overnight stay in Puri.

Day 3: Visit the UNESCO-listed Konark Sun Temple, an architectural masterpiece. Afterward, return to Bhubaneswar for your return flight to Mumbai, marking the end of your 3-day Odisha journey.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
