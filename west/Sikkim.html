Mumbai to Sikkim: 5-Day Himalayan Spiritual & Adventure Tour

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual, Adventure, and Nature Exploration

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Gangtok from Mumbai. Begin your spiritual journey by visiting the Rumtek Monastery, one of the most significant monasteries in Sikkim. Later, relax at Tsomgo Lake and enjoy the stunning views. Evening at leisure for shopping and local exploration. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 2: Early morning drive to Nathula Pass (Indo-China border) to witness breathtaking snow-capped views. Visit the sacred Baba Mandir, dedicated to an Indian soldier. In the evening, return to Gangtok and indulge in local cuisine. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 3: Wake up early to catch a glimpse of the majestic Kanchenjunga, the world’s third-highest peak, from a viewpoint. Spend the day exploring the local villages, culture, and craft markets. You’ll also visit the Enchey Monastery, a major Buddhist site. Overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 4: Drive to Yuksom, the gateway to trekking in Sikkim. Visit the Dubdi Monastery, the first Buddhist monastery in Sikkim, and soak in the spiritual atmosphere. Explore the surrounding landscapes and enjoy the tranquility of nature. Overnight stay in Yuksom.

Day 5: Visit the Temi Tea Estate, known for its world-class tea and scenic beauty. Enjoy a tea-tasting session and explore the lush tea gardens. Afterward, return to Gangtok for your departure, bringing with you memories of a spiritual and adventure-filled journey in the Himalayas.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
