Mathura and Vrindavan: 3-Day Spiritual Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual and Cultural Exploration

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Mathura from Delhi. Visit **Krishna Janmabhoomi**, the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Explore the **Dwarkadhish Temple** and the local ghats on the Yamuna River. Spend the evening at the **Vishram Ghat**, where Lord Krishna is believed to have rested. Overnight stay in Mathura.

Day 2: Early morning visit to **Vrindavan**, the land of Lord Krishna’s childhood. Visit the **Banke Bihari Temple**, one of the most famous temples in Vrindavan. Explore **Radha Raman Temple** and the serene **Seva Kunj**. Experience the evening **Holi festival rituals** at **Vrindavan’s temples**. Overnight stay in Vrindavan.

Day 3: Start your day with early morning **Radha Krishna Darshan** at the temples. Explore the peaceful **Prem Mandir** and enjoy the beautiful spiritual vibes. After lunch, return to Delhi, reflecting on the divine experiences and memories of Mathura and Vrindavan.

  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Banaras, Ayodhya, and Prayagraj: 3-Day Spiritual Temple Tour

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual and Temple Exploration

Start City: Any City in India

End City: Any City in India

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Varanasi. Start your spiritual journey by visiting the world-famous **Kashi Vishwanath Temple**, dedicated to Lord Shiva. Take a boat ride on the Ganges River at sunrise, followed by visits to **Dashashwamedh Ghat** and the ancient **Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple**. Explore the vibrant local markets and enjoy the city's spiritual atmosphere. Overnight stay in Varanasi.

Day 2: Travel to **Ayodhya**, the birthplace of Lord Rama. Visit **Ram Janmabhoomi**, **Hanuman Garhi**, and the ancient **Kanak Bhavan**. Immerse yourself in the rich history and spirituality of the city. In the afternoon, travel to **Prayagraj** (Allahabad). Upon arrival, visit the **Triveni Sangam** for a spiritual dip, and explore **Kumbh Mela Ghats** and **Anand Bhavan**. Overnight stay in Prayagraj.

Day 3: Begin the day with an early visit to **Bharat Mata Mandir** and **Prayagraj Fort**. Explore the rich cultural heritage of the city and pay homage at the sacred **Sangam Ghat**. In the evening, return to your departure city, carrying divine blessings and cherished memories from your spiritual journey.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.

Lucknow and Varanasi: 3-Day Cultural and Spiritual Journey

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Spiritual, Heritage Exploration

Start City: Lucknow

End City: Varanasi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Lucknow and begin your cultural exploration of the city, famous for its Mughal and Awadhi heritage. Visit **Bada Imambara**, **Chota Imambara**, and **Rumi Darwaza**, and indulge in authentic **Awadhi cuisine**. Overnight stay in Lucknow.

Day 2: Early morning drive to Varanasi. Visit **Kashi Vishwanath Temple**, enjoy a **Ganga Aarti** at **Dashashwamedh Ghat**, and explore **Sarnath**. Experience a boat ride on the Ganges at sunrise. Overnight stay in Varanasi.

Day 3: Visit **Banaras Hindu University**, **Kashi Vishwanath Corridor**, and **Tulsi Manas Mandir**. Shop at **Assi Ghat** before departing for your onward journey from Varanasi.
