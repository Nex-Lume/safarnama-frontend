Family Fun Jaipur Cultural Retreat

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Family Adventure and Cultural Experience

Start City: Jaipur

End City: Jaipur

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Jaipur and check in to a family-friendly heritage hotel. Visit Hawa Mahal and local markets for handicrafts.

Day 2: Explore the Amber Fort, enjoy an elephant ride, and experience the light and sound show in the evening.

Day 3: Visit Jaigarh and Nahargarh Forts. Enjoy a picnic with panoramic views of Jaipur.

Day 4: Discover Jantar Mantar and City Palace, followed by a cultural evening with traditional Rajasthani performances.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Type of Trip: Honeymoon Special

Start City: Jaipur

End City: Jaipur

Itinerary

Day 2: Explore Amber Fort with an elephant ride to the top. Visit Jaigarh Fort and the City Palace in the afternoon.

Day 3: Tour Jantar Mantar, shop at Johari Bazaar for jewelry, and stroll through the lush gardens of Sisodia Rani Ka Bagh.

Day 4: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and visit Albert Hall Museum before departing from Jaipur with wonderful memories.
