Enchanting Agra Getaway

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Heritage and Culture

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Agra. Visit the iconic Taj Mahal at sunset. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 2: Explore Agra Fort and take a day trip to Fatehpur Sikri. Return to Agra for the night.

Day 3: Visit the Itmad-ud-Daulah Tomb and enjoy shopping for marble crafts and souvenirs in the local markets. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 4: Morning at leisure. Depart for Delhi via Yamuna Expressway with beautiful memories.

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.

  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Family Fun in Agra

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Family Adventure

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Agra. Visit the Taj Mahal and enjoy a light-and-sound show at Agra Fort. Overnight stay in Agra.

Day 2: Explore Agra Fort and Mehtab Bagh. Spend time at a family-friendly park or enjoy a pottery workshop.

Day 3: Visit Fatehpur Sikri for an educational experience. Kids will love exploring Buland Darwaza and the ancient city ruins.

Day 4: Discover the Itmad-ud-Daulah Tomb, also known as the "Baby Taj," and shop for souvenirs in local markets.

Day 5: Relax in the morning. Depart for Delhi via the Yamuna Expressway with lovely family memories.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
