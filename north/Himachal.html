Himachal Wonders: Scenic Hills and Adventures

Duration: 6 Nights / 7 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Adventure, and Cultural

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Chandigarh, followed by a scenic drive to Shimla. Check into your hotel and enjoy a leisurely evening at Mall Road. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Visit Kufri, known for its panoramic views and adventure activities. Explore the Himalayan Nature Park and return to Shimla for sightseeing. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 3: Drive to Manali (approx. 7 hours). En route, enjoy views of Kullu Valley. Upon arrival, check into your hotel and relax. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Explore Solang Valley, famous for paragliding, skiing (seasonal), and other adventure sports. In the evening, visit Old Manali for cafes and culture. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 5: Enjoy a day trip to Rohtang Pass (subject to permit availability). Experience the snow-capped landscapes and enjoy activities like sledding. Return to Manali. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 6: Drive to Dharamshala (approx. 7 hours). Explore McLeod Ganj, visit the Dalai Lama Temple, and enjoy the serene surroundings. Overnight stay in Dharamshala.

Day 7: Visit Kangra Fort and enjoy views of the Dhauladhar Range. Drive to Chandigarh for your return flight to Mumbai with beautiful memories of Himachal.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Himalayan Majesty: Himachal Discovery

Duration: 7 Nights / 8 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Adventure, and Cultural

Start City: Chandigarh

End City: Chandigarh

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Chandigarh and take a scenic drive to Shimla (approx. 3 hours). Check-in and relax. Stroll along Mall Road and enjoy local delicacies. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Explore Shimla’s colonial charm, including **The Ridge**, **Christ Church**, and **Jakhoo Temple**. Proceed to Kufri for its stunning landscapes and activities like horse riding. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 3: Drive to Manali (approx. 7 hours). En route, visit **Pandoh Dam** and **Kullu Valley**. Upon arrival, unwind and explore the local market. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Experience adventure at **Solang Valley**, including paragliding and zorbing. If weather permits, take a day trip to **Rohtang Pass** for its snow-filled landscapes. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 5: Drive to Dharamshala (approx. 7 hours). Visit **McLeod Ganj**, the **Dalai Lama Temple**, and Bhagsu Waterfalls. Overnight stay in Dharamshala.

Day 6: Proceed to Dalhousie (approx. 6 hours). Explore **Khajjiar**, also known as "Mini Switzerland of India," and enjoy its lush meadows. Overnight stay in Dalhousie.

Day 7: Visit **Kangra Valley**, including **Kangra Fort** and **Masroor Rock Temples**. Enjoy panoramic views of the Dhauladhar Range. Overnight stay in Dharamshala.

Day 8: Return to Chandigarh (approx. 7 hours) for your onward journey, carrying back memories of Himachal’s breathtaking beauty.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Himachal Getaway: Nature and Tranquility

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic, Adventure, and Culture

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Start early in the morning from Delhi and drive to **Shimla** (approx. 8 hours). Arrive by evening, stroll along **Mall Road**, and enjoy views from **The Ridge**. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Visit **Kufri** for panoramic views and activities like horse riding and ziplining. In Shimla, explore **Jakhoo Temple**, **Christ Church**, and the **Viceregal Lodge**. Overnight stay in Shimla.

Day 3: Drive to **Manali** (approx. 8 hours). Enjoy scenic views of the valleys and rivers along the way. Spend the evening at leisure exploring **Old Manali** and **Manu Temple**. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Spend the day at **Solang Valley**, enjoying activities like paragliding, skiing (seasonal), and ATV rides. Visit **Rohtang Pass** (subject to permit availability) for snow-clad landscapes. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 5: Travel to **Dharamshala** (approx. 7 hours). Visit the **Dalai Lama Temple**, **Bhagsu Waterfalls**, and **McLeod Ganj Market**. Enjoy Tibetan cuisine and an overnight stay in Dharamshala.

Day 6: Morning drive to **Naddi Viewpoint** for a serene experience. Later, drive back to Delhi (approx. 10 hours) with unforgettable memories of Himachal Pradesh.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
