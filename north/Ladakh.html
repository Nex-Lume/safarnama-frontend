Ladakh 4-Day Adventure

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: High-altitude Adventure

Start City: Leh

End City: Leh

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Leh. Acclimatize to the altitude and explore Leh town. Visit Leh Palace and Shanti Stupa.

Day 2: Journey to Nubra Valley via the famous Khardung La Pass. Explore the sand dunes and visit the Diskit Monastery.

Day 3: Visit Pangong Lake, a stunning blue water lake surrounded by mountains. Enjoy the breathtaking views and return to Leh in the evening.

Day 4: Visit the famous monasteries of Leh such as Hemis, Thiksey, and Shey Monastery. Explore the local market before heading to the airport for departure.

Mumbai to Ladakh Adventure

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: High-altitude Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Leh

Embark on a thrilling 3-day adventure from Mumbai to the breathtaking landscapes of Ladakh. From high mountain passes to crystal-clear lakes, discover the awe-inspiring beauty of Ladakh while enjoying cultural insights and adventure activities.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Leh, the capital of Ladakh. Acclimatize to the high altitude and explore Leh Market. Visit Leh Palace and Shanti Stupa for a panoramic view of the city.

Day 2: Visit the spectacular Nubra Valley, crossing the Khardung La Pass (one of the highest motorable roads in the world). Explore the desert dunes and visit Diskit Monastery. Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.

Day 3: Journey to the famous Pangong Lake, known for its mesmerizing blue waters. Enjoy the breathtaking views and take some time to relax by the lake. Return to Leh in the evening for departure.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy a sunset cruise along the Mandovi River.
  • Sip on refreshing cocktails at beachside bars.
  • Dance at one of Goa’s famous beach parties.
  • Capture the vibrant colors of Goa’s street art and architecture.
  • Visit the renowned spice plantations of Goa.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in beach resorts and hotels.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • All transfers and sightseeing in a private vehicle.
  • Guided tours to famous attractions.
  • Water sports activities such as jet skiing and parasailing.

Romantic Ladakh Getaway for Couples

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Romantic Adventure

Start City: Leh

End City: Leh

Escape to the stunning landscapes of Ladakh for a romantic getaway. Explore high-altitude lakes, ancient monasteries, and enjoy peaceful moments amidst the serene beauty of the Himalayas.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Leh and check-in to a cozy hotel. Spend the day relaxing, enjoy a romantic dinner with a view of the mountains.

Day 2: Drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass. Explore the serene landscapes, enjoy a camel ride in the desert dunes, and visit Diskit Monastery. Overnight in Nubra.

Day 3: Journey to Pangong Lake, one of the most beautiful lakes in the world. Enjoy a peaceful evening by the lake with your partner. Return to Leh for an overnight stay.

Day 4: Visit the famous monasteries of Leh such as Hemis and Thiksey. Enjoy a romantic stroll around the Leh Market and have a traditional Ladakhi meal. Return to your hotel.

Day 5: Enjoy a farewell breakfast with panoramic views of the mountains before heading to the airport for your return journey.

Additional Highlights

  • Romantic candlelight dinner by the sea.
  • Couples’ spa experience.
  • Capture beautiful moments at scenic beaches.
  • Complimentary welcome cake and room decor.

Package Inclusions

  • Luxurious beachfront accommodations.
  • Daily breakfast and one romantic dinner.
  • Private vehicle for all sightseeing.
  • Guided tours for couples.
  • Watersports activities for couples.
