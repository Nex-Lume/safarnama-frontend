Golden City Amritsar Heritage Tour

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Heritage and Cultural Immersion

Start City: Amritsar

End City: Amritsar

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Amritsar and check into your hotel. Visit the iconic Golden Temple and participate in the evening prayer ceremony.

Day 2: Explore Jallianwala Bagh and learn about its historical significance. In the evening, visit the Wagah Border to witness the Beating Retreat Ceremony.

Day 3: Enjoy a food trail sampling Amritsari Kulchas and lassi. Visit Partition Museum and local bazaars for authentic handicrafts.

Day 4: Visit Ram Tirath Temple for a serene spiritual experience. Depart with unforgettable memories of Amritsar.

Amritsar Spiritual and Culinary Delight

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Solo Spiritual Journey with Culinary Adventures

Start City: Amritsar

End City: Amritsar

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Amritsar and settle into a peaceful boutique hotel. Visit the Golden Temple for a serene evening prayer and experience the Langar community meal.

Day 2: Embark on a guided heritage walk through the old city, discovering historic sites and bustling bazaars. Savor local dishes like Amritsari fish and kulchas.

Day 3: Visit the Partition Museum to learn about the region’s history. In the evening, explore local artisan workshops and try your hand at Phulkari embroidery.

Day 4: Take a day trip to the Wagah Border for the Beating Retreat Ceremony. End the day with a visit to Harike Wetlands for bird watching and tranquility.

Day 5: Conclude with a food trail exploring street delicacies. Take home spices and local sweets before departing with cherished memories.
