Madhya Pradesh Getaway: 4-Day Cultural and Wildlife Experience

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural Exploration and Wildlife

Start City: Bhopal

End City: Bhopal

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Bhopal. Begin your journey with a visit to the historic Sanchi Stupa, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Later, explore the prehistoric rock shelters of Bhimbetka. Overnight stay in Bhopal.

Day 2: Travel to Kanha National Park. Enjoy an evening wildlife safari in the lush forests, home to Bengal tigers, leopards, and more. Overnight stay near the park.

Day 3: Head to Khajuraho to explore the magnificent temples adorned with intricate carvings, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Overnight stay in Khajuraho.

Day 4: En route back to Bhopal, visit the historic Orchha Fort complex. Enjoy a scenic drive and capture the charm of Madhya Pradesh before departing from Bhopal in the evening.

Madhya Pradesh 3-Day Escape: Culture, Nature, and Adventure Package

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Heritage, Nature, and Adventure

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Depart from Mumbai and arrive in Bhopal via an early flight or train. Check into your hotel. Visit the renowned **Sanchi Stupa**, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, to explore its ancient Buddhist architecture and history. In the evening, enjoy a leisurely walk along **Upper Lake**, a serene spot in Bhopal. Overnight stay in Bhopal.

Day 2: Begin the day with a visit to the **Bhimbetka Rock Shelters**, known for prehistoric cave paintings and archaeological significance. Continue to **Bhojpur**, famous for its incomplete Shiva temple and massive Shiva Linga. Spend the evening exploring Bhopal’s bustling local markets for handicrafts and souvenirs. Overnight stay in Bhopal.

Day 3: Start the day at **Van Vihar National Park**, a protected habitat for a variety of wildlife, including tigers and leopards. After enjoying the park, return to your hotel for checkout. Post lunch, depart from Bhopal for Mumbai by flight or train, bringing home memories of Madhya Pradesh’s rich heritage and nature.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
