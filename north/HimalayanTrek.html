Himalayan Trekking Adventure: Annapurna Circuit

Duration: 13 Nights / 14 Days

Type of Trip: Trekking and Adventure

Start City: Kathmandu

End City: Kathmandu

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Kathmandu. Explore UNESCO World Heritage sites and prepare for the trek. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 2: Drive to Besisahar, the starting point of the Annapurna Circuit. Enjoy the scenic ride and overnight stay in Besisahar.

Day 3: Begin trekking through lush valleys and riverside trails to reach Tal, a serene village. Overnight stay in Tal.

Days 4-7: Trek through Chame, Pisang, and reach Manang. Acclimatize to the altitude and explore the picturesque village of Manang.

Day 8: Cross Thorong La Pass (5,416m), the highest point of the trek. Enjoy breathtaking views of the Annapurna range. Overnight stay in Muktinath.

Days 9-10: Descend to Jomsom and Marpha, known for its charming apple orchards and local culture. Overnight stays en route.

Day 11: Reach Tatopani and relax in the natural hot springs. Overnight stay in Tatopani.

Day 12: Trek through scenic trails to Ghorepani, a popular stopover for panoramic Himalayan views. Overnight stay in Ghorepani.

Day 13: Early morning trek to Poon Hill for a stunning sunrise over the Himalayas. Return to Pokhara for an evening of relaxation.

Day 14: Drive back to Kathmandu. Shop for souvenirs and enjoy a farewell dinner. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Mumbai to Himalayan Trekking Adventure: Everest Base Camp

Duration: 13 Nights / 14 Days

Type of Trip: Adventure, Trekking

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Kathmandu. Check into your hotel and enjoy a guided tour of Kathmandu’s UNESCO World Heritage sites, including Durbar Square and Swayambhunath Stupa. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 2: Take a scenic flight to Lukla (2,800m), the gateway to Everest. Begin your trek to Phakding (2,652m), passing through lush green valleys. Overnight stay in Phakding.

Day 3: Trek to Namche Bazaar (3,440m), a vibrant Sherpa town and the main trading hub in the Everest region. Acclimatize here with stunning views of the Himalayas. Overnight stay in Namche.

Day 4: Spend the day acclimatizing in Namche. Visit the Sherpa Museum and enjoy short hikes to nearby viewpoints. Overnight stay in Namche.

Day 5: Trek to Tengboche (3,860m), home to the famous Tengboche Monastery. Enjoy panoramic views of Mt. Everest, Ama Dablam, and other peaks. Overnight stay in Tengboche.

Day 6: Continue trekking to Dingboche (4,410m), a quaint village surrounded by majestic peaks. Overnight stay in Dingboche.

Day 7: Trek to Lobuche (4,940m), passing through Khumbu Glacier and memorial sites dedicated to climbers. Overnight stay in Lobuche.

Day 8: Reach Everest Base Camp (5,364m), the ultimate goal of the trek. Witness the incredible Khumbu Icefall and enjoy breathtaking surroundings. Return to Gorakshep for an overnight stay.

Day 9: Hike to Kala Patthar (5,545m) for a stunning sunrise view of Everest. Begin the descent back to Pheriche (4,280m). Overnight stay in Pheriche.

Days 10-12: Trek back to Lukla via Namche Bazaar, retracing your steps through picturesque villages. Celebrate the completion of the trek. Overnight stays en route.

Day 13: Fly back to Kathmandu. Spend the day exploring the city or shopping for souvenirs. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.

Day 14: Fly back to Mumbai with unforgettable memories of your Himalayan adventure.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Himalayan Basics: Short Trekking Adventure

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Adventure and Nature Exploration

Start City: Dehradun

End City: Dehradun

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Dehradun by flight or train. Meet your guide and transfer to the hill station of Mussoorie (1.5 hours). Relax and explore Mall Road. Overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day 2: Drive to Pantwari village (3 hours). Begin your trek to Nag Tibba Base Camp (2,600m), passing through forests and scenic meadows. Overnight camping under the stars.

Day 3: Trek to the summit of Nag Tibba (3,022m) for panoramic views of the Himalayan ranges. Descend to Pantwari and drive back to Mussoorie. Overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day 4: Visit **Dhanaulti Eco Park**, a serene forested area known for its tranquility. Enjoy short nature walks and return to Mussoorie. Overnight stay in Mussoorie.

Day 5: Travel to Rishikesh (2.5 hours). Enjoy white-water rafting on the Ganges and visit iconic spots like **Lakshman Jhula** and **Parmarth Niketan Ashram**. Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 6: Drive back to Dehradun (1.5 hours) and depart for your onward journey with cherished memories of the Himalayas.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.
