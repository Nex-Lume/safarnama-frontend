Rishikesh: 3-Day Spiritual and Adventure Experience

Duration: 2 Nights / 3 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual and Adventure Exploration

Start City: Rishikesh

End City: Rishikesh

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Rishikesh, enjoy a tranquil yoga session, and explore Laxman Jhula and Triveni Ghat. Evening Ganga Aarti. Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 2: Go white-water rafting on the Ganges, followed by an afternoon of trekking or exploring the Beatles Ashram. Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 3: Visit the Neelkanth Mahadev Temple nestled in the mountains, and return to Rishikesh for your departure.

Haridwar to Rishikesh and Kedarnath: 5-Day Spiritual Expedition

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Spiritual, Cultural, and Adventure Exploration

Start City: Haridwar

End City: Haridwar

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Haridwar. Visit Har Ki Pauri and witness the Ganga Aarti. Explore Mansa Devi Temple. Overnight stay in Haridwar.

Day 2: Travel to Rishikesh. Visit Laxman Jhula and Ram Jhula. Participate in yoga or Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat. Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 3: Travel to Kedarnath (via helicopter or trekking). Visit Kedarnath Temple and explore the area. Overnight stay in Kedarnath.

Day 4: Return to Rishikesh. Relax by the Ganges or indulge in a spa session. Overnight stay in Rishikesh.

Day 5: Explore Rishikesh's ashrams and temples before returning to Haridwar for departure. Visit Bharat Mata Temple before leaving for your onward journey.
