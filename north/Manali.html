Mumbai to Manali: 5-Day Himalayan Escape

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Adventure, Nature, and Relaxation

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Mumbai

Itinerary

Day 1: Depart from Mumbai by flight/train to Delhi. On arrival, take a scenic overnight bus or private car journey to Manali. Check in to your hotel upon arrival and enjoy a leisurely day exploring **Mall Road** and nearby attractions. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 2: Begin your day by visiting **Hadimba Temple**, an ancient wooden temple surrounded by deodar trees. Explore **Manu Temple** and relax at **Vashisht Hot Springs**. Spend the evening in Old Manali, known for its cafes and vibrant atmosphere. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 3: Head to **Solang Valley**, known for its adventure activities. Enjoy paragliding, zorbing, and cable car rides. In winter, indulge in skiing and snow activities. Return to Manali and enjoy free time to shop for souvenirs. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Visit the breathtaking **Rohtang Pass** (subject to permit availability) for panoramic views of the Himalayas and snow-covered landscapes. If the pass is inaccessible, visit **Gulaba** or **Kothi Village** as alternative scenic spots. Return to Manali for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Check out from your hotel and begin your journey back to Delhi. From Delhi, catch a flight/train to Mumbai, marking the end of your memorable trip.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Manali Adventure and Serenity: 5-Day Mountain Getaway

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Adventure, Nature, and Relaxation

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Manali after an overnight journey from Delhi. Check into your hotel and enjoy a relaxing day. Visit the bustling **Mall Road**, shop for local handicrafts, and explore cozy cafes. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 2: Explore Manali’s key attractions. Visit the ancient **Hadimba Devi Temple**, **Manu Temple**, and **Vashisht Village** with its famous hot springs. Spend the evening enjoying Old Manali’s unique vibe with live music and riverside cafes. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 3: Spend the day in **Solang Valley**, known for thrilling adventure activities. Experience paragliding, zip-lining, and quad biking. During winters, enjoy skiing and other snow activities. Return to your hotel for a cozy evening. Overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Embark on a scenic drive to **Rohtang Pass** (subject to permit availability). Admire the snow-covered peaks and enjoy activities like snowboarding and sledging. Alternatively, visit **Atal Tunnel** for a unique experience. Return to Manali for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Spend your final morning by the **Beas River**, enjoying its serene beauty. Check out from your hotel and begin your journey back to Delhi, taking with you unforgettable memories of Manali.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
