Explore Delhi from Mumbai

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Cultural, Historical, and Shopping

Start City: Mumbai

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Fly from Mumbai to Delhi. Arrive in Delhi and explore **Qutub Minar** and **Humayun's Tomb**. End the day with a visit to **India Gate** and enjoy the evening at **Connaught Place**. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 2: Visit **Jama Masjid** and explore the bustling lanes of **Chandni Chowk**. Take a rickshaw ride through the lanes, and shop for handicrafts and spices. Visit **Raj Ghat**, the memorial of Mahatma Gandhi. In the evening, visit the **Lotus Temple** and **Lodhi Gardens** for a romantic stroll. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 3: Explore the best shopping hubs in Delhi such as **Dilli Haat** and **Khan Market** for traditional crafts and souvenirs. In the evening, visit **Jantar Mantar** and **Rashtrapati Bhavan**. Enjoy dinner at a rooftop restaurant with a view of the city. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 4: Visit **Red Fort**, a UNESCO World Heritage site, and explore **Jama Masjid**. Later, visit **National Museum** and **India Habitat Centre** for some culture and history. Enjoy a relaxing evening at **India Habitat Centre**. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 5: Visit **Kingdom of Dreams** or attend a live performance. Take a walk around **Hauz Khas Village** for vibrant cafés and art galleries. Explore **Agrasen ki Baoli** and spend the evening at **Dilli Haat** for local handicrafts and food. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 6: Explore the local markets for souvenirs and enjoy a final meal in Delhi before heading to the airport for your flight back to Mumbai.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.

Romantic Escape to Delhi

Duration: 4 Nights / 5 Days

Type of Trip: Honeymoon Special

Start City: Delhi

End City: Delhi

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Delhi. Start with a visit to **Qutub Minar** and **Humayun's Tomb**. End the evening with a romantic dinner at **India Gate**, followed by a peaceful boat ride in **Yamuna River**. Overnight stay in a luxury hotel.

Day 2: Visit the serene **Lotus Temple** and **Lodhi Gardens** for a peaceful morning walk. In the afternoon, explore **Chandni Chowk** and enjoy the bustling streets with your partner. Visit **Jama Masjid** in the evening and end your day with a candlelight dinner at **Connaught Place**. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 3: Visit the **Red Fort** and explore the majestic **Rashtrapati Bhavan**. Enjoy a couple’s walk at **India Gate** and spend quality time in the **Lodi Gardens**. In the evening, explore **Hauz Khas Village**, known for its romantic ambiance and cafés. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 4: Start your day with a visit to the artistic **Kingdom of Dreams** or enjoy a day trip to **Neemrana Fort Palace** for a royal experience. In the evening, attend a live performance or cultural show for an unforgettable evening. Overnight stay in Delhi.

Day 5: Explore the famous **Dilli Haat** for handicrafts and souvenirs. Enjoy a romantic shopping spree before departure. Head to the airport with beautiful memories of your romantic Delhi escape.
