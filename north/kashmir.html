Mesmerizing Kashmir Retreat

Duration: 5 Nights / 6 Days

Type of Trip: Scenic Adventure

Start City: Srinagar

End City: Srinagar

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar. Shikara ride on Dal Lake and a visit to Mughal Gardens. Overnight stay at a houseboat.

Day 2: Day trip to Gulmarg. Enjoy gondola rides and skiing. Return to Srinagar for the night.

Day 3: Drive to Pahalgam. Explore Betaab Valley and Aru Valley. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.

Day 4: Enjoy leisure activities like horse riding and hiking. Return to Srinagar in the evening.

Day 5: Day trip to Sonmarg. Visit Thajiwas Glacier. Return to Srinagar for overnight stay.

Day 6: Visit local markets for shopping and depart from Srinagar with unforgettable memories.

Additional Highlights

  • Enjoy an Ayurvedic massage in Munnar or Kovalam.
  • Stay in a luxurious houseboat on Alleppey's backwaters.
  • Experience sunset at Kovalam Beach.
  • Trek through lush green tea gardens and spice plantations.

Package Inclusions

  • Accommodation in premium hotels and resorts.
  • Daily breakfast and select meals.
  • Private transfers and sightseeing.
  • Guided tours at all destinations.
  • Houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
